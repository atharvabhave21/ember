# /ember:reach-out

Draft a first-touch outreach message to a contact in the Notion Contacts database.

Usage: `/ember:reach-out [Name]`

Looks up the contact, reads the user's Ember Settings, infers the best hook and tone, and drafts one copy-paste ready message for the chosen channel (LinkedIn DM, LinkedIn InMail, Email, or WhatsApp).

Behaviour is defined in `skills/02-outreach.md`.
