# /ember:pipeline

Show a complete overview of your job search pipeline at the company level.

Displays all target companies (Is Target = true) grouped by Overall Status:
Active → Targeting → Researching → Passed

For each company shows: contacts there, open applications, last interaction date.
Flags stale companies with ⚠️ and surfaces the single most important action to take.

Example: `/ember:pipeline`

Behaviour is defined in `skills/10-pipeline.md`.
